About Carolina Code School

Our Mission

At Carolina Code School, we mentor individuals into professional programmers in an immersive classroom setting. Located in Greenville, South Carolina, we offer a full-time, 12-week Web Development course designed to prepare students for a career in software development.

Our Instructors

Our instructors are industry experts and passionate educators who are committed to the success of every single student. Our courses are structured to provide in-person, one-on-one mentorship and guidance.

Our Curriculum

Our structured curriculum is designed with input from Upstate employers to give students a full-stack foundation and to prepare them for careers in technology - right here at home, or wherever their career path may go.

Immersive Web Development Course

Our full-time course curriculum was developed by expert programmers and technical teams who know what it takes to be a professional web developer. Twelve weeks of hands-on instruction can take you from newbie to professional and get you ready for a career in software development - in the Carolinas or way, way beyond.
